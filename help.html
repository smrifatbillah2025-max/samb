Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a new attendance sheet?
    Click on "Create New Sheet" from the home page, fill in the required details (class name, subject, etc.), and click "Create Sheet".
  • How do I mark attendance?
    Open any saved sheet, click on the student's name to toggle between Present/Absent. Changes are saved automatically.
  • Can I print the attendance records?
    Yes! Open any sheet and click the "Print" button. The system will generate a print-ready format.
  • Is my data secure?
    Absolutely! All data is securely stored using Firebase authentication and cloud database with industry-standard encryption.
  • How do I enable dark mode?
    Use the dark mode toggle switch in the header. Your preference will be saved automatically.
  • Can I use this on mobile devices?
    Yes! The application is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices including smartphones and tablets.
